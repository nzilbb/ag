Stanford POS Tagger

Annotator that tags words with their part of speech (POS) according to the Stanford POS Tagger developed by the Stanford Natural Language Processing Group.

When this annotator is installed, it downloads models of version 4.2.0 of the Stanford POS Tagger from https://nlp.stanford.edu/software/stanford-tagger-4.2.0.zip, which requires access to the internet.

Alternatively, it's possible to specify your own version of the tagger models at installation time.
